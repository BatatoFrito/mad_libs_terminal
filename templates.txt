Instructions: To add a new story, write in a new paragraph:
#[Title]Story text... (without the #)
(Attention: A title needs to have more than 1 character and has to be unique)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To add word spots in your story, simply add 4 underlines where you want it to be.
#[Title]Story text ____... ____... (without the #)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Word spots cannot be inside another word:
#[Title]Story te____xt... (without the #) <- Not Allowed
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Also, you can make a story become a comment simply by inserting # right before the title
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
